About MIS

Better young Montanans, through physical education.

Founded in 2022 by Ali Behnam and JD Stephenson, the Montana Institute of Sport is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to improving physical education in Montana's public schools through curriculum support, teaching grants, and professional development.

Tackle Montana is one of MIS's direct-support programs — putting elite coaching directly into schools at no cost.
