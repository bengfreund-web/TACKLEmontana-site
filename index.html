Questions

Frequently asked questions.

Is Tackle Montana really free? +

Yes. No cost to your school, athletic department, players, or families — ever. Fully funded by the Montana Institute of Sport.

How long are sessions, and what do they cover? +

45 minutes, on-field, led by our coaching staff — body position, tracking, head placement, and control in the contact zone.

Is it just one session? +

Not necessarily. Most programs start with one session, and many bring us back for follow-up coaching as the season goes on.

Do we need pads or full contact gear? +

No. Sessions run in practice gear, with a low-impact option that builds technique without taking players to the ground.

Who can request a session? +

Any Montana football program — middle school, high school, or club — plus PE departments and coaches.

How far in advance should we schedule? +

Submit the form and JD Stephenson will follow up to lock in a date around your team's calendar.
