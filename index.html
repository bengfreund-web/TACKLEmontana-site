About MIS

Better young Montanans, through physical education.

Founded in 2022 by Ali Behnam and JD Stephenson, the Montana Institute of Sport is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit improving physical education across Montana's public schools through curriculum support, teaching grants, and professional development. Tackle Montana is one of the ways MIS puts that mission on the field.

Best fit: football programs, PE departments, middle schools, high schools, and coaches who want a simple, credible way to improve tackle teaching without adding cost or complexity.

Questions

Frequently asked questions.

Is Tackle Montana really free? +

Yes. There is no cost to your school, athletic department, players, or families — ever. The session is fully funded by the Montana Institute of Sport.

How long is the session, and what does it involve? +

One 45-minute, on-field session led by our coaching staff, focused on rugby-based tackle fundamentals: body position, head placement, tracking, and footwork.

Do we need pads or full contact gear? +

No. Sessions can run in practice gear, and we also offer a low-impact option that builds technique without taking players to the ground.

Who can request a session? +

Any Montana football program — middle school, high school, or club — along with PE departments and coaches looking to improve tackle teaching.

How far in advance should we schedule? +

Submit the form and JD Stephenson will follow up to lock in a date, typically within the next two months, built entirely around your team's calendar.
