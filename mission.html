Our Mission

We make better football players.

Launched in 2025, our mission is to elevate football-style tackling with rugby technique — building confidence, know-how, and control around contact.

How We Coach

Skillset meets technique.

Through high-level professional coaching, athletes combine their existing skillset and physicality with targeted cues and drills designed to improve tackle success.

Every session is led by coaches who've played and coached rugby at the international level — bringing fundamentals refined at the sport's highest tiers directly to Montana football programs.

Coach Jed Holloway with the Gallatin Raptors

Low-Impact Option

Risk reduction, without losing the reps.

Not every training session needs full contact. For teams looking to minimize injury risk or run lower-intensity practices, we focus on refining technique and body position without taking players to the ground.
